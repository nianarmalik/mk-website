How I Work

The Vibe Coding Method

A creative loop between human intuition and AI execution — from raw idea to shipped product.

💡

Catch the Vibe

An idea hits. Could be a problem I'm annoyed by, something missing in the world, or just a spark of curiosity.

🗣️

Describe It

Turn the vibe into a prompt. No spec documents — just a clear description of what the thing should feel like.

🤖

AI Builds It

Claude Code, Cursor, or ChatGPT does the heavy lifting. I steer, review, and iterate. Think of it as pair programming with a tireless AI.

🚀

Ship Fast

Deploy it. Show it to people. Collect real feedback. Don't wait for perfection — vibes don't wait.

Philosophy

Why Vibe Coding?

Vibe coding is about lowering the barrier between imagination and reality. AI doesn't replace the creative mind — it removes the friction.

Instead of spending weeks fighting boilerplate and syntax, I spend that energy on the parts that actually matter: the idea, the design, the user experience.

Every app here was an experiment. Some are polished products, some are chaotic proofs-of-concept. All of them were fun to make — and that's the whole point.
